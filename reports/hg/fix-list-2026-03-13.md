## Root Cause Analysis

The score dropped from 83 to 72 because the audit now crawls 50 pages (up from a smaller sample). Of these 50, **32 are pages/ and collections/ URLs** that Cloudflare's bot protection blocks entirely. The crawler sees only the "Just a moment..." challenge page instead of the actual content.

**This means:** The 64 critical issues and ~128 of the warnings are NOT real content problems — they are crawlability problems caused by Cloudflare. The underlying pages likely have proper titles, meta descriptions, and H1 tags that are invisible to both our crawler and search engine bots.

The remaining 18 pages (products + homepage + contact) score 90-95/100 and have genuinely good SEO, with the exception of missing alt text on template-level images.

| # | Header | Status | Recommended Fix | Expected Impact |
|---|--------|--------|-----------------|-----------------|
